About Dr. Sharma's Care

Dr. Neha Sharma

Welcome to Dr. Sharma's Care! Founded by Dr. Neha Sharma, a compassionate and highly skilled medical professional, our clinic is dedicated to providing personalized and holistic healthcare solutions tailored to each patient's unique needs.

With over 12 years of experience in general medicine and wellness, Dr. Sharma is committed to improving the health and wellbeing of her community through expert diagnosis, innovative treatments, and preventive care.

Education & Qualifications

  • MBBS - All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi (2007 - 2012)
  • MD in General Medicine - Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER), Chandigarh (2012 - 2015)
  • Certified in Lifestyle Medicine - American College of Lifestyle Medicine (2020)
  • Advanced training in Diabetes Management, Hypertension, and Preventive Care

Professional Experience

Dr. Sharma has worked in various renowned hospitals and health centers across India, including:

  • Senior Resident at Fortis Hospital, New Delhi (2015-2017)
  • Consultant Physician at Max Healthcare, Gurugram (2017-2020)
  • Founder & Lead Physician at Dr. Sharma's Care Clinic (2020 - Present)

Her expertise spans across internal medicine, chronic disease management, women's health, and preventive healthcare, ensuring a comprehensive approach to patient care.

Our Mission

At Dr. Sharma's Care, our mission is to empower patients through education, compassionate care, and evidence-based treatments. We believe in fostering a collaborative environment where patients feel heard, respected, and supported in their health journey.

Community Involvement

Dr. Sharma is deeply involved in community health initiatives, organizing free health camps, wellness workshops, and awareness drives about lifestyle diseases and mental health. She is also an active speaker at national health conferences and contributes regularly to medical journals.
